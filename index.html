Sustainability & Smart Infrastructure

New Port City is built on the foundation of environmental responsibility and technological innovation. Our infrastructure incorporates:

  • 100% renewable energy sources powering the entire city
  • Smart grid technology for optimal energy distribution
  • Advanced water recycling and conservation systems
  • Green building standards for all structures
  • Over 40% of land dedicated to green spaces and natural habitats
