Our Journey

New Port City was envisioned as a groundbreaking smart city project, combining cutting-edge technology with sustainable urban planning.

New Port City reaches its planned capacity, fully realizing the vision of a sustainable, smart urban environment.

Location & Master Plan

Strategic Location

New Port City is strategically located on the coast, offering easy access to major transportation hubs and trade routes. Our location combines the benefits of urban living with the beauty of coastal landscapes.

  • 30 minutes from International Airport
  • Direct access to high-speed rail network
  • Connected to major highways
  • Proximity to natural reserves and beaches

Our comprehensive master plan ensures balanced development, with dedicated zones for residential, commercial, industrial, and recreational areas. The plan prioritizes green spaces, efficient transportation, and sustainable infrastructure.
